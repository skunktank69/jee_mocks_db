    Comment : In this question we will not get a unique function \(\mathrm{g}(\mathrm{x})\), but in order to match the answer we will have to assume \(g(x)=\frac{\mathrm{xe}^{\mathrm{x}} \sin ^{-1} \mathrm{x}}{\sqrt{1-\mathrm{x}^2}}\).
