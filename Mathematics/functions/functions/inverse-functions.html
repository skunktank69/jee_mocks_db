Q1.

The range of \(f(x)=4 \sin ^{-1}\left(\frac{x^{2}}{x^{2}+1}\right)\) is

  1. \[[0,2 \pi]\]
  2. \[[0,2 \pi)\]
  3. \[[0, \pi)\]
  4. \[[0, \pi]\]

Answer Key

  1. Q1: B

Explanations

  1. Q1: \( \begin{aligned} & \frac{x^2}{1+x^2}=1-\frac{1}{1+x^2}<1 \\\\ \therefore & 0 \leq \frac{x^2}{1+x^2}<1 \\\\ \Rightarrow & 0 \leq \sin ^{-1}\left(\frac{x^2}{1+x^2}\right)<\frac{\pi}{2} \\\\ \Rightarrow & 0 \leq 4 \sin ^{-1}\left(\frac{x^2}{1+x^2}\right)<2 \pi \end{aligned} \)
