Q1.

Which among the following pairs has only herbicides?

  1. Aldrin and Dieldrin
  2. Sodium chlorate and Aldrin
  3. Sodium arsinate and Dieldrin
  4. Sodium chlorate and sodium arsinite

Answer Key

  1. Q1: D

Explanations

  1. Q1: Both sodium chlorate and sodium arsenate behave as herbicide.
